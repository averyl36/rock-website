About Us

Welcome to Camp Rock, the premier pet rock sitting service here in Las Cruces, NM. We've all heard of the concept of dog daycare, but how about one for your special little pebbles? We know you cherish your little boulder, and when in our care, we want to cherish them as our own. At Camp Rock, we promise to give your pet rock the time of its life each time they visit.
